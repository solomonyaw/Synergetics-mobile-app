18

The day thou gavest, Lord, is ended

9898

ZUVA ramatipa rapera.
Dima rinodzika zvino
Mangwanani nziyo taimba
Yerisa zororo redu.

 

Sangano renyu harirari
Nyika ichiona mwenje
Richitarisa kwese kwese
Pasina kuzororazve.

 

Kunyika dzese nezvitsuwa.
Zuva rimwe richiuya
Minamato hainyarari
Nokukurumbidzwa kwenyu.

 

Zuva renyu riri kumutsa
Hama dzokumadokera
Miromo yese inoita
Mabasa enyu anzike.

Ndzizvo Tenzi umambo hwenyu
Hauzoperi senyika
Umambo hwenyu hunokura
Zvisikwa zvese zvikudze.
