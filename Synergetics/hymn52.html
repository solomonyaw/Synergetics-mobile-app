HYMNS

52

Forty days and Forty nights

7777

MAZUVA nousiku
Aya makumi mana
Wakaziya kugwenga
Uchirunzirwa Tenzi.

 

Wakapiswa ngezuva,
Wakatota ngebeto,
Wakagara nemhuka,
Wakatsamira mahwe.

 

Hatinzwi shungu here,
Nokurega kufara?
Titsanye tinamate
Tinzwe nhamo saiwe.

 

Satan’ achirunzira
Nyama yedu nomweya
Iwe mukundi wake
Tibatsire tikunde.

 

Ndizvo tinonyaradzwa,
Tinofadzwa zvikuru;
Ngerosi dzinopenya
Dzinobatsira isu.

 

Tichengete M’ponisi;
Tigare pedyo newe;
Tizoonekwe newe
Ngemusi wokumuka.
