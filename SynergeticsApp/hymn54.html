HYMNS

54

Lord Jesus, think on me

7475

NDIFUNGE Tenzi Jesu,
Ndanyangadza,
Ndisuke muzvitadzo
Ngeropa rako.

 

Ndifunge Tenzi Jesu,
Ndiri wako.
Mubasa rangu rese
Undibatsire.

 

Ndifunge Tenzi Jesu,
Ndine nhamo.
Mukurwa mira petyo
Undikanire.


Ndifunge Tenzi Jesu,
Ndarasika.
Penyisa pane dima
Ndione nzira.

 

Ndifunge Tenzi Jesu,
Gara neni.
Yasvika mhandu yangu
Undiponise.


Ndifunge Tenzi Jesu,
Pakuguma.
Ndirege ndikuone
Undifarise.
