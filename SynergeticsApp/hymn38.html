HYMNS

38

See amid the winter snow

8888D

MWANA wabarirwa isu.
Ona arimo muchando,
Nyenye inoyera kwazvo
Yakatsidzwa karekare.
Zuva rakakomborerwa !
Ngatiimbe ngekufara
Kristu arimo mudanga,
Nhasi wabarirwa isu.

 

Iye wakasika denga,
Anogara achikudzwa
Ngengerosi dzese dzese,
Arimo mudanga zvino;

Imi vanhu tiudzei
Nyaya yenyu yokufara
Ngei maregera hwai
Dziri kure pamakomo?


Apo takafudza hwai
Takaona mbiri huru
Ngerosi dzaimba kuti:
“Runyararo kuna vanhu.”

 

Jesu sheshe inoyera
Uno rudo rwashamisa
Waregera mbiri yako
Uchiuya somurombo.

Mwana wedu unodiwa
Tipe titevere Iwe
Mukutiwa nokupfava,
Rudo, tsitsi, nourombo.
