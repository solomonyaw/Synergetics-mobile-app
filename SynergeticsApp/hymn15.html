HYMNS

15

Hail, gladdening Light

Irereg.

TINOKUDZA iwe zuva redu
Uri kubuda kubva kunaBaba;
Kupenya kwokupenya,
Kristu Tenzi Unoyera.

 

Zuva repasi riri kudoka zvino,
Mwenje yamadeko yaoneka,
Tikuddze Baba, Mukorore,
Newe Mweya Unoyera.


Ifaniro yedu kukuimbira Iwe,
Panzvimbo dzese napanguva dzese:
Mwari waMwari, tsime roupenyu hwedu,
Pasi ngapatunhidze Tenzi zvino narini.
