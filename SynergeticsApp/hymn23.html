On Jordan’s bank the Baptist’s cry

8888

MUBABATIDZI paJordan
Ari kusima masoko;
Izwai ‘zvo anoreva,
“Mambo Mukuru wasvika.”

 

Bvisai zvitadzo zvese,
Chenisai mwoyo yenyu;
Ngaive umba dzaMwari
Dzinodadza kushanyirwa.”

Ndiwe Tenzi Muponisi,
Shangu yedu, tuso redu;
Tisine tsitsi tinofa
Samaruva akauna.

 

Ponisa vatenda Tenzi,
Sunungura vanyangadzi,
Veneka ngembiri yako,
Uite zvese zvinake.

 

Mbiri ngaive kunewe,
M’ponisi wavanhu vese;
Mbiri kunaMwari Baba
Naye Mweya Unoyera. Amen.
